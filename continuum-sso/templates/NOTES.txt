=== Important Notes ===
1. Configure your Keycloak client with the following redirect URI:
   {{ ternary "https" "http" .Values.oauth2Proxy.cookie.secure }}://{{ .Values.oauth2Proxy.publicHost }}/oauth2/callback
2. Store Keycloak client credentials in a Kubernetes Secret and reference it via:
   oauth2Proxy.keycloak.existingSecret
3. Configure your ingress controller to use oauth2-proxy for authentication:
   annotations:
     nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/auth-url: "http://{{ include "continuum-sso.fullname" . }}-oauth2-proxy.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local:4180/oauth2/auth"
     nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/auth-signin: "https://{{ .Values.oauth2Proxy.publicHost }}/oauth2/start?rd=$escaped_request_uri"
